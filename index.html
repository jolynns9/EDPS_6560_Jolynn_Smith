About Me

I am a master’s student of Instructional Design and Educational Technology at The University of Utah.

I have spent the last 5 years doing project management work at FranklinCovey both in their International Education Department and their Innovations Department. Before that, I worked for a non-profit that helped mentor entrepreneurs. I am extremely organized, but I have found that I love creating effective and beautiful trainings. In all my projects, I found myself seeking out opportunities to help develop content that would inspire learning. That is why I decided to pursue a degree in Instructional Design.

I value hard-work, transparency, collaboration and respect. I am excited to assist you in developing quality trainings that meet your needs.
